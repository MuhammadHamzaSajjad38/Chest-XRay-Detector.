Automated Diagnosis of X-Ray Images Using Deep Learning

Leveraging state-of-the-art deep learning to provide fast, accurate, and explainable analysis of chest X-rays. An FYP by students of the University of Mianwali.

Launch the Detector Tool

Key Features of Our System

High Accuracy

Utilizes the advanced Xception architecture, fine-tuned on a vast dataset to achieve high diagnostic accuracy for multiple diseases.

Explainable AI (XAI)

Generates Grad-CAM heatmaps to visualize the regions the AI focused on, providing transparency and building trust in the results.

Instant Results

Our optimized model and streamlined web interface deliver a classification and visualization in seconds, not minutes.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between advanced artificial intelligence and practical medical diagnostics.

This project was born from a desire to create a tool that can genuinely assist medical professionals. By automating the initial screening of chest X-rays, we aim to reduce workload, speed up diagnosis, and ultimately contribute to better patient outcomes, especially in resource-limited settings.

A Simple 3-Step Process

1. Upload X-ray

Select or drag-and-drop a chest X-ray image into the detector tool.

2. AI Analysis

Our Xception model processes the image to identify key pathological features.

3. Get Diagnosis

Receive an instant classification and a heatmap highlighting important areas.
