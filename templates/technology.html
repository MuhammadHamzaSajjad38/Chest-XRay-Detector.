The Technology Behind Our AI

From custom architecture to explainable results, here's a look under the hood.

Custom CNN Architecture

Our model is a custom-designed Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) heavily inspired by the powerful Xception architecture.

Instead of using a generic pre-trained model, our network is tailored specifically for the nuances of Brain MRI scans. The core components are **Depthwise Separable Convolutions**, which are highly efficient at learning features from images. This allows the model to be both powerful and less prone to overfitting—a critical advantage when working with medical data.

Explainable AI (XAI) with Grad-CAM

We believe AI should not be a "black box." That's why we integrated Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping).

This technique produces a visual heatmap that overlays the original X-ray, highlighting the exact regions the model found most important for its diagnosis. This provides invaluable transparency, allowing medical professionals to verify that the model is "looking" at the correct pathological areas, thereby building trust and confidence in its predictions.
