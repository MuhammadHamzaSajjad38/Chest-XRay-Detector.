About Our Project

An initiative to harness the power of artificial intelligence to aid in the rapid diagnosis of critical respiratory diseases.

The Challenge

Respiratory diseases like COVID-19, Pneumonia, and Tuberculosis are a significant global health threat. Rapid and accurate diagnosis from chest X-rays is critical for timely treatment and controlling outbreaks. However, manual interpretation can be time-consuming and requires expert radiologists, who may not always be available, especially in underserved areas. An automated AI system can act as a powerful tool to assist medical professionals by providing fast, reliable, and data-driven insights.

Our Objectives

  • To develop a deep learning model for multi-class classification of chest X-rays into four categories: COVID-19, Pneumonia, Tuberculosis, or Normal.
  • To implement the high-performance Xception architecture using transfer learning for superior accuracy.
  • To create a user-friendly web application for easy analysis of X-ray images.
  • To integrate Grad-CAM to produce visual heatmaps, making the AI's diagnostic focus transparent and understandable to clinicians.

Project Workflow

1. Data Collection

Aggregated and prepared thousands of X-ray images from public datasets.

2. Model Training

Trained the Xception model using TensorFlow, leveraging transfer learning.

3. Evaluation & Tuning

Rigorously tested and fine-tuned the model to maximize accuracy and reliability.

4. Web Development

Built this responsive web application using Flask to serve the model.
